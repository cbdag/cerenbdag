Research Areas

Quantum Many-Body Dynamics

Explore the non-equilibrium behavior of quantum systems, thermalization, many-body localization, and quantum chaos in interacting systems.

Quantum Information and Scrambling

Investigate the spread of quantum information in complex systems using tools such as out-of-time-order correlators (OTOCs), with connections to black holes and quantum processors.

Spinor Condensates and Topological Phases

Study emergent phenomena in spinor Bose-Einstein condensates, focusing on topological order, quantum scars, and dynamical phase transitions.
